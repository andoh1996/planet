Covenant Partnership

“Now he who supplies seed to the sower and bread for food will also supply and increase your store of seed and will enlarge the harvest of your righteousness.” 2 Corinthians 9:10

  • How do I become a Covenant Partner?

    •Make a monthly financial commitment of any amount to this Foundation.

    •Agree to support KOM Foundation outreach project with in-kind commitments (Food, Clothes, Building Materials etc.)
    This is ideal for professionals and college students.

  • Your monthly donations will help us to:

    - To Aid in The Wel-Fare of Children in Ghana and Abroad by Providing Love, Care, Food and Education.
    - To Aid in The Welfare of Prisoners in Ghana.
    - To Provide Instructional Seminars in Community Centers, Schools, Youth Centers on Empowering the Youth.
    - To Support Our “Suicide Is Not an Option” Radio Program for All Ages but with emphasis on the youth.
    • Give scholarships to needy to attend school.

  • Benefits:

    • • A personal Partner in foundation membership certificate signed.
    • Opportunities to join the KOM Foundation for all outreach projects and activities.
    • Personal access to Prayer Worrier for prayer and encouragement.

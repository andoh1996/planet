Join Us Today

Planet Women empowers women across three dynamic sectors—Creative Arts/Culture, Sports, and Agribusiness—to drive economic growth and gender equality. In Creative Arts and Culture, where women make up a significant portion of participants but are constrained by lack of funding and often earn less than men, our programs help women turn their creative talents into sustainable income. In Sports, where women make up about 25% of athletes in competitive fields globally, we promote inclusion and leadership opportunities. In Agribusiness, a sector where over 50% of women work in sub-Saharan Africa yet have limited access to land and resources, we support women to build profitable, sustainable enterprises.


  • Upcoming projects

State of the art Women’s Arts Center

A Women’s Arts Center would empower female artists by providing training, resources, and a dedicated space (including an ultra modern music/recording studio) to develop their skills , showcase their work, and build sustainable careers. Through networking, exhibitions, and entrepreneurship support, the centre would foster collaboration, economic independence, and cultural preservation for women in the creative arts.



Targeted date of completion: On or before December 2030

African Hair Salon and Fashion Hub

Setting up an African Hair Salon and Fashion Hub will serve as a vibrant centre for celebrating and promoting African beauty and style while economically empowering women. This hub will provide training in hairdressing, styling, and fashion design, equipping women with skills to create thriving businesses. By offering a platform to showcase African hairstyles, textiles, and designs, alongside access to local and global markets, it will elevate women as leaders in the beauty and fashion industries, driving cultural pride and economic growth.



Targeted date of completion: On or before December 2030

Film Village for Women in movie production

A dedicated film village for women movie producers will create opportunities for women to produce, showcase, and distribute their work, driving economic empowerment and career advancement in the film industry. With access to resources, training, and networking, the village will enable women to build profitable careers, amplify their voices in media, and inspire the next generation of female filmmakers.



Targeted date of completion: On or before December 2030

Women Jewellery and Kente Hub

We aim to establish a jewellery and kente hub dedicated to empowering women in the jewellery industry through beading, lapidary work, and gemstone crafting. By providing women artisans with resources and training to produce high-quality, finished products, the hub will equip them to lead the industry and build sustainable businesses. With established marketing channels, including opportunities for export, women will gain access to global markets, fostering economic independence, and creating a network of skilled female entrepreneurs in the craft and textile sectors.



Targeted date of completion: On or before December 2030
